## Root-cause evidence

The tester journal records the EA's `SP500.DWX` market orders at the exact
broker-D1 timestamp as rejected with `[Market closed]`, including
2018-11-22, 2018-12-12, 2019-02-06, 2019-05-30, 2019-06-14, and 2021-05-19.
The same six rejected attempts recur in both deterministic runs.

The implementation marks `g_strategy_entry_attempted = true` and persists the
daily attempt flag before calling `QM_TM_OpenPosition`. A market-closed broker
rejection therefore consumes the Card's one-shot entry even though the Card
defines entry at the first **executable** quote within 900 seconds of the D1
boundary. The observed behavior is consistent with an execution-lifecycle
mismatch, not evidence that the MAC(5) signal has or lacks economic edge.
