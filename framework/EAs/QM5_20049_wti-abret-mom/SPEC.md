# QM5_20049 — WTI Abnormal-Return Momentum

On the first H1 bar of a broker day, compare the prior completed D1 open-to-close
return with a causal 252-D1 mean and sample standard deviation. Enter in the
same direction beyond two standard deviations, use a 2.5 ATR(20,D1) stop, and
flatten at 10:00 broker time or after 36 hours. Q02/backtest only.
