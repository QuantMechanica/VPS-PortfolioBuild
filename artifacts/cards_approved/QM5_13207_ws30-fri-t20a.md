## Locked Entry Logic

On each Friday, using DST-correct `America/New_York` time:

1. Evaluate only the first tick of the M15 bar beginning 13:30 New York.
2. Use `WS30.DWX` observed M15 bars only. The newest price is the close at
   shift 1 and the oldest price is the close at shift 1921.
3. Require exactly 1921 available completed closes and both endpoint closes to
   be finite and strictly positive. Any history/read failure rejects entry.
4. Compute `return_20d = close(shift=1) / close(shift=1921) - 1` and enter only
   when `return_20d > 0`. Zero and negative returns reject entry.
5. The 1921 samples span 1920 observed M15 bar intervals. This is deliberately
   not 20 calendar days, not 20 D1 bars, not an SMA, and not a wall-clock
   subtraction. Missing market/session buckets do not become synthetic bars.
6. If accepted, buy at market. Compute the simple average of true range over
   the 56 completed M15 bars ending at shift 1 and place a hard stop exactly
   `1.0 * ATR56` below the actual entry. There is no take profit.

The trend gate is entry-only. It must never suppress position management,
same-day 16:00 New York liquidation, or immediate liquidation after a New York
date rollover.

## Locked Exit And State Logic

- Close at 16:00 New York.
- If a position survives into a later New York date, flatten immediately.
- Never re-enter on the same New York date, including after restart or stop.
- One position per symbol and registered magic.

No short, overnight hold, add-on, trailing stop, break-even, partial close,
grid, martingale, pyramiding, PnL adaptation, year exclusion, external feed,
or ML is allowed. In particular, 2020 was excluded from model selection only;
the production mechanic contains no 2020 or regime-year veto.
