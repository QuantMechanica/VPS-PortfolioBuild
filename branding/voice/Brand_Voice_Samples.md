# QuantMechanica — Brand Voice Samples

**Core Principle:** First-person singular ("I") — one engineer, Build in Public. **Never "We".**

Five voice attributes: **Honest · Data-Driven · Self-Ironic about failures · Technical but accessible · "I" voice**

---

## DO — How we sound

### Data-driven, direct
> "Out of 369 strategies, 34 survived. That's an 8% pass rate. Here's what killed the other 92%."

> "I ran 8,237 backtests through ten validation phases. 236 passed. 15 are live on DarwinexZero."

### Honest about uncertainty
> "I don't know yet if this portfolio will work live. The backtests say yes, but backtests have lied to me before."

> "The pipeline has held up so far. Ask me again in 90 days."

### Technical but accessible
> "Walk-forward analysis tests your strategy on data it has never seen. If it only works on the data it was trained on, it's memorizing, not learning."

> "Probability of Backtest Overfitting — PBO — tells you how likely you got lucky. If PBO is above 50%, your edge is probably noise."

### Self-ironic at failures (Brand Favorite)
> "I lost €10,000 on a grid bot. I was greedy, I was naive, and I ignored risks I didn't want to think about. That's it."

> "SM_423 looked brilliant in the sweep. Ran perfectly in walk-forward. Zero trades in live forward-test. The filter was too tight. I'm rebuilding it."

### First-person / Build in Public
> "Every result gets published. The winners. The losers. The ones I had to rebuild three times."

> "I'm posting everything because that's the deal: you see the process, or you don't trust the results."

## DON'T — How we do NOT sound

### No hype
~~"This EA will revolutionize your trading! Proven 95% win rate!"~~

### No guru tone
~~"Follow my system and you too can achieve financial freedom in 90 days."~~

### No corporate "We"
~~"We leverage cutting-edge AI technology to deliver superior risk-adjusted returns."~~
~~"Our team has developed..."~~

### No weak/apologetic
~~"I'm just a small trader, but maybe my approach could potentially work..."~~

### No false precision
~~"This strategy has a 73.42% win rate and will earn you $1,847.50 per month."~~

## Content Formulas

**Stat Hook:** `[big number]. [unexpected fraction]. [what that means].`
- "618 EAs. 97% failed. Here's the 3% that didn't."

**Failure Post:** `[what I tried]. [what happened]. [what I learned]. [what's next].`
- "I thought news filters would help. They killed 40% of trades. PBO jumped to 62%. I'm rethinking the whole approach."

**Milestone Post:** `[what changed]. [one number]. [honest caveat].`
- "V5 portfolio went live on demo today. 5 strategies, ENB 2.93. I'll know in 30 days whether that ENB holds."
