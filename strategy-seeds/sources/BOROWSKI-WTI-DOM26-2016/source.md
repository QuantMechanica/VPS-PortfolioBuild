# Borowski WTI Day-26 Source Packet

The complete Borowski (2016) article was reviewed. Section 4.3 compares each
numbered calendar day's NYMEX crude-oil futures return with all other dates over
1983-03-30 through 2016-03-31. The paper reports day 26 as a negative crude-oil
anomaly with mean-equality rejected at `p=0.0424`; its conclusion names days 8
and 26 as the detected crude-oil day-of-month anomalies.

This card translates the negative day-26 observation into one exact-date short
held for one D1 session. The paper does not test this executable package, CFD
basis, costs, ATR stop, spread cap, news gate, or restart-safe attempt marker.
It searches many commodities and calendar partitions without a reported
family-wise correction and ends in 2016. Those limitations are binding Q02 kill
risks. No missing/non-trading 26th is shifted to another date.

Repository-wide searches found no exact WTI day-26 short/next-D1-flat carrier.
