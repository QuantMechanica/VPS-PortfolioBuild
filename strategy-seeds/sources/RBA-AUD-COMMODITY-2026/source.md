## Why This Source Is Valid

This is the canonical source for the `xng-aud-rspread` card. The RBA explains
that commodity prices, including natural gas, influence Australia's terms of
trade and the Australian dollar exchange rate. The card uses that single
mechanism source to define a deterministic Darwinex-native spread between
`XNGUSD.DWX` and `AUDUSD.DWX`.

No source performance claim is imported. Q02 and later phases must validate or
reject the mechanical basket.
