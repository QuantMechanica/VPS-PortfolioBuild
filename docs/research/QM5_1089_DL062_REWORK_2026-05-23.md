## 4. Diagnosis

Three overlapping defects, identical in shape to QM5_1088 with one extra wrinkle:

1. **Harness mismatch (primary)** — RAA Robust Pairs allocates *within each pair* using
   per-asset TMOM/MA rules. A single-symbol work_item sees only one leg of one pair, so
   the pair-balance step cannot execute. Even if the per-asset rules fire correctly on
   that one chart, the EA cannot construct the dual-momentum decision the card
   specifies. Sibling diagnoses: [[QM5_1048_DL062_REWORK_2026-05-23]],
   [[QM5_1088_DL062_REWORK_2026-05-23]], [[QM5_1044_DL062_REWORK_2026-05-23]].
2. **Dispatcher fanout** — per [[project_qm_dispatcher_universe_mismatch_2026-05-23]],
   29 of 42 rows ran on symbols (Aussie/CAD/CHF/GBP crosses, UK100, XAGUSD, XNGUSD)
   that the card never targeted. These rows are dispatcher noise inflating the 0.829
   zero-trade pct.
3. **`min_trades_required = 36` is wrong for monthly-rebalance** — the card says ~12
   trades/year/symbol; a 36-trade floor demands the EA fire on a frequency the source
   explicitly does not. Even a working basket build would FAIL the gate as configured.
4. **MetaTester hangs on SP500.DWX + USDJPY.DWX** — only two in-universe symbols hung
   (vs more for QM5_1088); the other 4 in-universe legs (XAUUSD, XTIUSD, EURUSD, NDX)
   completed but zero-traded. Lower hang count suggests the EA's blocking pattern is
   sensitive to specific symbol data — root-causing should follow the same trail as
   QM5_1088 but with the SP500.DWX vs NDX.DWX divergence as the differential signal.

The 0.829 zero-trade rate decomposes as: 29 dispatcher-fanout rows (structurally zero
by routing), 4 in-universe TMOM/MA legs that genuinely zero-traded because the pair
context was missing, 2 hung in-universe runs. **None of the 42 rows is evidence the
strategy doesn't work.**

## 5. Recommended change vector

**DO NOT relax entry conditions or substitute signal logic.** The TMOM > 0 + MA > 0
rule is the *whole* edge claim from Alpha Architect; weakening either turns this into a
naked long-bias and breaks R2/R4.

Same three structural options as QM5_1088:

- **(a)** Off-tester per-asset decision-file ingest (monthly TMOM/MA computed off
  broker D1 closes, written to `D:\QM\strategy_farm\artifacts\rank\QM5_1089_<YYYYMM>.csv`).
- **(b) RECOMMENDED** — Multi-symbol-from-one-chart: anchor on one routable symbol
  (NDX.DWX preferred for live; SP500.DWX viable for backtest only), pull D1 closes for
  all 8 pair legs via `CopyRates`, compute the 4 pair allocations in-process, place
  per-magic positions on each leg. Reuses the QM5_10717 basket-EA wrapper
  ([[project_qm_basket_ea_build_2026-05-22]]).
- **(c)** Declare DEAD-by-harness, lift to fresh basket-EA card.

Whichever path: the dispatcher whitelist fix from
[[project_qm_dispatcher_universe_mismatch_2026-05-23]] is required, and
`min_trades_required` must scale to the EA's stated cadence (12/yr ≈ 12 per 1-year
work_item; basket-level min ≈ 60 across 4 pairs × 12 mo). Until then, even (b) will
re-fanout and re-FAIL the gate.
