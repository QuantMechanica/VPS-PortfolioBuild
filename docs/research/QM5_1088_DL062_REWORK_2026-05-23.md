## 4. Diagnosis

Three overlapping defects, none of them strategy-side:

1. **Harness mismatch (primary)** — same shape as
   [[QM5_1048_DL062_REWORK_2026-05-23]] and [[QM5_1044_DL062_REWORK_2026-05-23]]. FAA
   RAVC needs all 7 universe symbols loaded simultaneously to compute the composite
   rank. The MT5 strategy tester runs one symbol per work_item; cross-sectional rank
   cannot fire, so even the 7 in-universe rows would zero-trade by construction.
2. **Dispatcher fanout** — per [[project_qm_dispatcher_universe_mismatch_2026-05-23]],
   the work_items dispatcher ignored `target_symbols` and fanned the EA across 30
   off-universe instruments (Aussie/CAD/CHF crosses, UK100, WS30, XAGUSD, XNGUSD…).
   These 30 rows inflate the 0.882 zero-trade ratio with structural noise.
3. **MetaTester hangs on the in-universe runs** — the few in-universe attempts didn't
   even complete; they hung (`METATESTER_HUNG + REPORT_MISSING + INCOMPLETE_RUNS`).
   `min_trades_required=36` is also inconsistent with `expected_trades_per_year_per_symbol=12`
   for a monthly-rebalance EA — that gate is calibrated for a daily-signal strategy.

The 0.882 zero-trade rate is **entirely infrastructure**: harness + routing + tester
hang + miscalibrated min-trades threshold. Zero of the 37 rows constitutes evidence
about the *strategy*.

## 5. Recommended change vector

**DO NOT relax entry conditions or substitute signal logic.** Entry/exit are not the
defect surface. This is a multi-symbol TAA strategy executed in a single-symbol harness,
with a broken dispatcher on top.

Same three structural options as QM5_1048; same recommendation:

- **(a)** Off-tester ranking + decision-file ingest (sibling job writes monthly rank
  CSV; EA reads it and acts only when `Symbol()` is on the list). R4-clean, mechanical,
  back-replayable from a committed rank history.
- **(b) RECOMMENDED** — Multi-symbol-from-one-chart pattern: anchor the EA on one
  universe symbol (e.g. SP500.DWX for backtest; NDX.DWX for live since SP500.DWX is not
  broker-routable), pull D1 closes for the other 6 via `CopyRates(symbol, PERIOD_D1, …)`,
  rank in-process, place per-magic legs on the selected top-3. Slots into the existing
  single-work_item P2 wiring. Use the QM5_10717 basket-EA wrapper as the structural
  template ([[project_qm_basket_ea_build_2026-05-22]]).
- **(c)** Declare structural incompatibility, mark `pipeline_phase: DEAD` with a
  Lessons-Learned note, lift the thesis into a fresh basket-EA card.

Whichever path: also requires the dispatcher `target_symbols`-whitelist fix from
[[project_qm_dispatcher_universe_mismatch_2026-05-23]]; without it, even (b) will be
re-enqueued against 30 wrong-universe FX rows next cycle. The `min_trades_required`
formula must also accept monthly-rebalance EAs (`12 trades/yr × 7 yr ≈ 70 → bar of ~12`,
not 36).

The METATESTER hangs on NDX/EURUSD/SP500/USDJPY/XAUUSD/XTIUSD/GDAXI are a separate
infra ticket — likely tied to the EA blocking on rank-from-other-symbols data that the
single-chart harness never delivers; (b) should make them moot but the hang itself
should be reproduced and root-caused, not assumed away.

## 6. Q08 / Q11 risks (carry-forward for any reworked card)

- Q08: 7-symbol → top-3 concentration; the universe is heavily equity-correlated
  (SP500/NDX/GDAXI plus pro-cyclical XTIUSD). In risk-off the absolute-momentum gate
  forces all 3 slots to cash — that *is* the source's claimed protection, but it makes
  the strategy's worst-case calendar segments structurally trade-free, not failing.

  total. Per-symbol-per-year P3 reporting is misleading because trade allocation across

- Source caveat for (b) live path: SP500.DWX backtest-only — promotion needs the NDX
  anchor + correlation-rank computed off whatever live-routable proxy is chosen, with
  T6 parallel-validation.
