## 4. Diagnosis

**Primary defect is routing, not signal.** The card declares a 3-symbol US-cash-session
universe; the enqueue broadcast across the full DXZ FX/index basket. FX pairs and
European/UK indices have no 16:00-NY/09:30-NY cash-session anchor, so the entry rule
can never fire there. The 22 FX "zero-trade fails" are false-positive evidence: they
inflated the DL-062 trigger from a strategy signal into a routing artefact.

**Secondary, real strategy-level signal**: SP500.DWX *did* zero-trade on the
P3/year=2024 run with `min_trades_required=30`. On the correct symbol, the 20-session
overnight-minus-intraday rolling filter likely sat negative through much of 2024
(intraday-rebound regime, post-Fed-pivot). That is a *filter calibration* issue, not a
core-thesis failure.

## 5. Recommended change vector

**DO NOT relax raw entry conditions.** That hint mis-fits this case — relaxing on the
incompatible-symbol rows would not increase trades there (no cash session = no signal,
ever) and would corrupt the strategy on its native universe.

1. **PRIMARY — ops fix at the enqueue layer (OPS_FIX_REQUIRED for codex):**
   pin P2 enqueue to `target_symbols` whitelist when the card declares one. Without
   this, every session-anchored EA will keep tripping DL-062 falsely. Tracks a recurring
   class of defects, not just this card. *Not in scope for this research task — file an
   ops_issue.*
2. **SECONDARY — strategy variant family (RECYCLE this card) — 3-variant fan:**
   - **V1 (baseline-no-filter)**: drop the 20-session overnight-minus-intraday gate;
     keep spread filter + FOMC/CPI blackout. Establishes the unfiltered raw edge.
   - **V2 (slow-filter)**: replace 20-session with 60-session rolling gate. Tests the
     hypothesis that the filter horizon is too short and chops the EA off during
     regime transitions.
   - **V3 (positive-week filter)**: require prior-week overnight-return positive (5-bar
     binary), instead of trailing-mean condition. Cheaper, less regime-flippy.
   Run all three only on `target_symbols`. Falsification: if V1 still zero-trades or
   loses on SP500/NDX/WS30 across 2018-2024 P3 OOS, the thesis is dead; if V1 fires
   normally and V2/V3 sit at higher trade counts than the original, the filter was
   strangling — calibrate.
3. **SIGNAL SUBSTITUTION is NOT recommended.** The mechanism (close-to-open premium)
   is in the literature consistently; the V5 implementation is faithful. The defect
   is filter calibration, not the indicator/edge identity.
