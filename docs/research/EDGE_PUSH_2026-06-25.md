### What the 6 actually share — the survival RECIPE
1. **Reputable, specific, cited source** (Grimes / Connors / named MQL5 & TradingView scripts) —

2. **A structural level, not a bare indicator trigger** — market structure, OHLC structure,

4. **ATR-normalized risk + defined exits** — ATR stops, R-multiple targets (1.5–2R), time exits,

5. **Multi-timeframe context** — higher-TF trend/structure gate + lower-TF entry.
6. **Moderate frequency (24–100/yr)** — not HFT scalps, not ultra-rare.

**The ~2690 deaths lack ≥1 of these** — single-indicator triggers, no structure, no

## B. OWNER's idea — Opening-Range / Prev-Day-High Breakout (Trend, INTRADAY)

This fits the survival recipe AND fills the real gap (it's **intraday, EOD-flat** — a holding
period none of the 6 swing survivors have → structurally low correlation to them).

Provenance (R1): Crabel "Opening Range Breakout"; Zarattini & Aziz (2023) 5-min ORB on QQQ
showed strong risk-adjusted results with proper filters. Mechanical (R2), data available for
indices (R3), no ML (R4).

### Faithful spec (the fidelity that separates it from a dying naive version)
- **Instruments:** NDX.DWX, SP500/WS30.DWX, GER40.DWX (index CFDs — clean cash-session opens,
  where ORB works; SP500.DWX is backtest-only → live leg = WS30/NDX).
- **Session window in BROKER TIME (the make-or-break detail):** define the *cash-session open*
  per index and convert to Darwinex broker time (GMT+2 outside US DST / GMT+3 during US DST).
  US indices: 09:30 ET open. DAX: 09:00 CET. Encode the open + a fixed opening-range window
  (e.g. first 15–30 min) — DO NOT use a 24h "daily" high; use the *session* range.
- **Entry:** break of the opening-range high (or prior-day high if it sits above the OR) on a
  *completed* M5/M15 bar within the first N minutes after the OR forms. Long on OR-high break,
  short on OR-low break.
- **Confirmation filter (kills fakeouts):** relative-strength / range-expansion gate — e.g.
  the breakout bar's range > k·ATR(14) AND today's pre-break range > prior-day same-window
  range (volume proxy on CFDs). Skip if no expansion.
- **Stop:** ATR-based behind the opposite OR extreme, capped at ~1.5–2·ATR.
- **Target / exit:** trail or 1.5–2R, **hard EOD time-exit** (flat before session close → no
  overnight gap risk). Time-stop if no follow-through within M bars.
- **One position/pending per magic; news blackout (framework rule); RISK_FIXED backtest.**
- **Freq:** ~120–200 trades/yr/symbol (1 session/day, ~50–80% trade days) → high enough for
  robust stats, an asset for Q08.

### Why this is a strong portfolio sleeve
Intraday-flat + breakout behavior + index instruments = a sleeve whose return stream should be
near-uncorrelated to the swing MR/trend survivors → directly attacks the "DD too high, too few
sleeves" portfolio problem.
