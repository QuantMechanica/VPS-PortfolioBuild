## 4. Diagnosis

**Structural harness mismatch, not a strategy defect.** This is a **basket-rotation
EA** — it needs all 4 universe symbols loaded simultaneously to compute the
cross-sectional rank, then trades only the top-N. The MT5 strategy tester here is
running the EA against **one symbol per work_item**. With a 4-symbol universe reduced
to a single chart symbol, the EA cannot rank cross-sectionally and cannot fire any
trade. Worse: every row is on a symbol that isn't even in the universe, so the EA's
internal `Symbol() in {NDX,WS30,GDAXI,UK100}` check (if present) would refuse to act,
and otherwise would still have nothing to rank.

Zero P2 rows targeted the actual card universe. The 90% zero-trade rate is 100%
infrastructure: 22 FX + 4 commodities × wrong-symbol × not-a-basket-harness.

## 5. Recommended change vector

**DO NOT relax entry conditions or substitute signal logic.** Entry/exit are not the
defect surface. The defect is that an inherently multi-symbol strategy was enqueued
into a single-symbol tester harness.

Three structural options for rework (OWNER decision needed before any cards-review
work):

- **(a) Off-tester ranking + decision-file ingest (cleanest, mechanical, R4-clean)**:
  a sibling daily job (semi-annual gate triggered at last-trading-day Jun/Dec) computes
  the rank from broker D1 closes, writes `D:\QM\strategy_farm\artifacts\rank\QM5_1048_<YYYYMM>.csv`.
  The EA reads it on `OnTick`/`OnTimer` and trades only when its `Symbol()` is on the
  list. Backtest-replayable from a checked-in rank history. Mechanically the same
  decision; only the place where ranking happens moves.
- **(b) Multi-symbol-from-one-chart MQL5 pattern**: EA placed on one anchor symbol (e.g.
  NDX.DWX) and pulls D1 closes for the other 3 via `CopyRates(symbol, PERIOD_D1, …)`,
  ranks in-process, and uses per-magic positions on the selected symbols. Standard MT5
  pattern. P2 still single work_item, but the work_item covers the whole basket. Needs a
  basket-EA wrapper similar to `[[project_qm_basket_ea_build_2026-05-22]]` /
  QM5_10717 reference. This is the recommended path because it slots into the
  existing factory work-item shape without a new harness.
- **(c) Declare structural incompatibility, kill the card as drafted, lift to basket-EA
  infra only**: mark the card DEAD-by-harness in `Lessons Learned`, port the thesis to a
  fresh basket-EA card aligned with the QM5_10717 single-host basket pattern.

**Recommendation: (b).** Smallest blast radius, reuses existing P2 wiring, keeps the
card alive. (a) is acceptable if the basket-EA wrapper proves brittle. (c) is the kill
path only if (b) costs more than starting over.

Note: this needs to be paired with the same **target_symbols-whitelist enqueue fix**
called out in [[QM5_10020_DL062_REWORK_2026-05-23]] and
[[QM5_1044_DL062_REWORK_2026-05-23]] — until that is in place, even (b) will be
re-enqueued against FX next cycle.
