# Friday Smoke Gemini 2026-05-22

## Strategy Direction: Commodity-Price-Driven FX Cross-Sectional Ranking

**Thesis:**
The structural link between commodity exporters (AUD, CAD, NZD) and their primary export markets (Gold, Oil, Dairy) provides a slow-moving fundamental anchor for FX relative value. By ranking these "commodity currencies" based on the rolling correlation and momentum of their respective benchmark commodities, we can identify periods of fundamental-price divergence.

**Mechanism:**
1. Compute a 20-day "Commodity Basis" for AUD (Gold), CAD (Oil), and NZD (Dairy).
2. Rank currencies by their divergence from the commodity-implied fair value.
3. Long the undervalued (high commodity momentum, lagging FX) and short the overvalued.
4. Filter by risk-on regime (AUD/CAD spread) to avoid commodity-crash contagion.

**Falsification:**
If the strategy does not outperform a simple momentum basket during periods of stable commodity trends, the commodity-link is non-additive and the strategy fails.
