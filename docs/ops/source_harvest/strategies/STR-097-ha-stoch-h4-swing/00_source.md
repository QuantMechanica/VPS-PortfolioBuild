## Stated rules (verbatim anchors)

- Indicators (post #1): "Heiken Ashi candles / 100 sma close / Stochs 8,3,3, low/high".
- Trend filter (post #1): "if the price is above the ma the trend is up and we only
  want to buy and below is down and we only want to sell".
- Entry long (post #2): uptrend with "a smooth decline in prices signified by green
  HA candles followed by red. When the HA candle turns back to green and the
  stochastics make a nice smooth cross towards the bottom of the stochs window then
  wait for the 4 hour candle to close and open your long trade." Short = "the opposite".
- Timeframe (post #1): "I like to use 4 hour charts" (others use other TFs).
- MM variants (post #2): (1) 50-pip SL, hold until HA flips against trend, trail
  stop; (2) 50-pip SL + 50-pip TP, SL→+1 pip after +25 pips; (3) 50-pip SL, take
  half at +25, remainder to break-even, trail, exit on HA flip (post #8 restates 3).
- Frequency (post #1): "usual to see 5 or more trades per week" (across the
  author's watchlist).
- Pairs demonstrated: EURCAD, EURAUD, USDCHF, GBPUSD (posts #2–#5).
- HA-doji (post #22, p.8): small-body HA candles with both wicks are "good reversal
  signals … worth watching out for" — an observation, NOT a stated entry/exit rule.
